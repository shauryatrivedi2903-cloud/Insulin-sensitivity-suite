Results & Interpretation
    • HOMA-IR = (Glucose × Insulin) / 405 — Higher → greater insulin resistance.
    • HOMA-BETA = (20 × Insulin)/(Glucose − 3.5) — β-cell function estimate.
    • QUICKI = 1 / [log(Insulin)+log(Glucose)] — Higher → better sensitivity.
    • Matsuda Index = 10000 / √(FPG × FPI × Mean G × Mean I) — Whole-body sensitivity.
    • ISI (0,120) = 10000 / √(G₀ × I₀ × G₁₂₀ × I₁₂₀) — Two-point estimate.
    • IGI = (Insulin₃₀ − Insulin₀)/(Glucose₃₀ − Glucose₀) — Early insulin response.
    • Disposition Index (DI) = IGI × Matsuda Index — β-cell function × sensitivity.
